# Privacy Policy — StandUp Anti-Bullying Toolkit

**Last updated:** 2026-04-26

## What we collect

**Nothing.**

The extension does not collect, transmit, or store any personally identifiable information. There is no analytics SDK, no telemetry, no remote logging.

## What is stored locally on your device

- Your quiz progress and last-seen tab (using `chrome.storage.local`).
- This data never leaves your browser. Uninstalling the extension deletes it.

## Permissions explained

- **`storage`** — to remember your quiz progress between sessions, on your device only.
- **`sidePanel`** — to open the "Learn / Story / Helplines" side panel.
- **No host permissions** — the extension does not read or modify any websites.

## External links

The extension links out to:
- The petition site (after you finish the quiz).
- Public helpline organisations (CHILDLINE, iCall, Vandrevala, KIRAN).
- Country-law source documents.

When you follow these links, the destination site's own privacy policy applies.

## Children

The extension is intended for general audiences including young readers. No personal data is collected from anyone, including children.
